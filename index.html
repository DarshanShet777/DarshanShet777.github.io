Programming

I've been programming for about 11 years now. I have dabbled into everything I've found related to programming. Some things that I could mention are Arduinos, Raspberry Pi's, Heroku, SQL servers, etc. Most importantly, I like to understand how computers and software work together. We're in a lucky era called the Digital Age where computers do most of our work. We can't live without them. So, learning about it is crucial if we're to live in this age. Though, to help people advance is my goal and computer programming is just a hobby.
